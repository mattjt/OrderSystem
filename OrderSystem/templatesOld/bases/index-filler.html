MORT 11 is a high school robotics team, based out of Flanders New Jersey. Founded in 1996, MORT is one of New Jersey's longest sustaining FRC teams.

FIRST - For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology

FIRST is an organization that promotes teamwork and collaboration among engineers. FIRST assists students to learn science and technology though robotics competitions.
